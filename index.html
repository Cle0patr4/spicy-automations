Everything you need to know about AI automation for your business.

What is AI automation and how can it help my business?

AI automation uses artificial intelligence, APIs, and modern AI models to automate repetitive business tasks. It can help your business by automating sales outreach, customer support, lead qualification, data entry, and operational workflows‚Äîsaving time, reducing errors, and allowing your team to focus on high-value work.

How much does a custom AI automation cost?

The cost varies depending on the complexity of your automation needs. We offer a $50 USD consultation where we analyze your specific process and provide a detailed custom quote. Most projects require a 50% upfront payment to begin, with the remaining 50% due upon delivery.

How long does it take to build an AI automation?

Timeline depends on the project scope. Simple automations can be delivered in 1-2 weeks, while more complex AI agents or multi-system integrations may take 4-8 weeks. We always deliver an MVP (Minimum Viable Product) first for testing and feedback before the final launch.

Do I need technical knowledge to use AI automations?

No technical knowledge is required. We handle all the technical implementation, and we train your team on how to use and manage the automation. Our solutions are designed to be user-friendly and integrate seamlessly with your existing tools.

What kind of support do you offer after the automation is built?

We offer ongoing monthly support agreements that include a dedicated automation expert who continuously monitors, optimizes, and updates your workflows as your business grows. Technology evolves, and we ensure your automations evolve with it.

What tools and technologies do you use for AI automation?

We use a combination of custom code, APIs, n8n workflows, and modern AI models including GPT, Claude, and other large language models. We also integrate with popular business tools like CRMs, email platforms, communication tools, and databases to create seamless workflows.
